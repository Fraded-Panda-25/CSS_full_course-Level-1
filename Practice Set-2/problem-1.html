Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an iconic Indian cricketer and former all-format captain, widely celebrated as the "Hitman" for his explosive batting and six-hitting prowess. He holds the record for the highest individual ODI score (264) and three ODI double-centuries. He led India to the 2024 T20 World Cup, the 2025 ICC Champions Trophy, and the Mumbai Indians to five IPL titles.
